ATLANTA LUXURY REAL ESTATE

This web application allows the ease of searching for a quality home in Atlanta. The user can use the search bar to select homes within a specific city. The filter options allow further search specifications. Click the "view listing" to navigate to the listing's detailed page which provides more information and a link to the page of quality real estate agents to take the next step in the home buying process. Three viewing options are available above the real estate listings by clicking on the icons. In addition, one can sign in and sign out of the web app using their Google account.
