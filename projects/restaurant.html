ATLANTA BISTRO RESTAURANT

Atlanta Bistro restaurant is a straight-forward web app for a steak house restaurant. It allows the visitors to easily have to the most information at first glance. This is built with React, and has a single file which stores the data. In the reviews section, one can click on the arrows to view several reviews of the restaurant. Also, one can click on the "view menu" button for an image of the menu.
